<h1>How I Started Supporting Arsenal</h1>
<img src="arsenalbadge.jpg"/>
<p>As mentioned above, I have been following The Premier League for almost 10 years. Growing up in Canada I did not have a lot of influence to get involved
in Football as it is not nearly as popular at home as it is in other parts of the world. I still remember the day that I fell in love with the sport. I had been
playing in an indoor winter league for a few years and wanted to watch a professional game, so I turned on the TV one Saturday morning and watch Manchester United
Vs Derby County. I believe the match ended 0-0 but what really spoke to me was what they showed during the half-time break, a montage of the Arsenal Invincibles.
Watching the way they played is what truly helped me appreciate the beautiful game for what it is.</p>
